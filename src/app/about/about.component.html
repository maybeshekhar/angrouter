About Us

We are a group of seasoned professionals united by the common vision of revolutionising micro-mobility through introducing sustainable models that are accessible, affordable and shareable for all. At driEV, we work towards achieving 3 SDG's - Affordable and Clean Energy (SDG 7), Sustainable Cities and Communities (SDG 11) and Climate Action(SDG 13). By addressing the needs of the public through a scalable, green and future-proof model, we hope we are doing our best to combat climate change and global warming and preserve our resources for tomorrow.

When every fuel-based bike that we ride adds to the carbon footprints and stays accountable for global warming, we are giving you an OPTION to choose between - Green Future and No Future.
